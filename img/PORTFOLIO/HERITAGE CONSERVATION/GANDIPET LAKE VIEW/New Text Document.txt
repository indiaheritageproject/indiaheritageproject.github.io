REVITALIZATION OF GANDIPET LAKE VIEW POINT Hyderabad-(bold)

site area: 600 Sft


The aim of this project is to protect and restore the character of a stone bund filled with soil and sand, this place is a sun set view point. The design intervention incorporates major structural issues to the existing stone bund, as it strives to respect and highlight the architectural qualities of the existing structure, such as the exposed concrete soffit, copping details or the unique irregular shape of the stone bund rising on high level.
