Dr Sudhir Naik Residence
Location: Gowliguda, Hyderabad-(bold)

site area: 5400 Sft


The goal of this project is to preserve the character of a century-old residency bungalow located close to banks of river musi by documenting its architecture, materials, historical context, and cultural significance. The documentation will include oral histories and photographs, and will serve as a valuable resource for future restoration and conservation efforts.
