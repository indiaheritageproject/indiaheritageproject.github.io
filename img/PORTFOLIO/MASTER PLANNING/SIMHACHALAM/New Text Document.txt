GHAT ROAD PROPOSALS FOR EXIT AT SIMHACHALAM DEVASTHANAM

The project's objective is to research and document the alignment of existing ghat roads, prepare a detailed project report using applicable IRC codes, and estimate  and design the  Ghat Alignment  with respect to traffic demand  forecast. Data  collection, alignment design,  design  drawings, and detail estimate  preparation are the methodology  for this project
